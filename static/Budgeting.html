Spending Needs are the expenses on items that are a must in your everyday lives. A few examples can be toilet paper, toothpaste, gas, rent, car insurance, phone bill, light and water bill, and much more.

Want expenses are purchasing items that are not necassary, items that you do not need to ensure a healthy and happy life style. For example: video games, legos, amongst other things and if they are not vital for your healthy survival it is a want.

Savings is money put aside for many reasons. These reasons can be to ensure you have money in case of an emergency; to save up for a vacation; to have enough money to retire and live comfortably; and even just to have money for financial stability.
